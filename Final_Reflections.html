My Reflections

Semester Reflection: Embracing Creativity and Self-Discovery Self

As we bid farewell to 2023, this semester stands out as a transformative chapter. Amidst the challenges and triumphs, I discovered a deeper understanding of myself, discovering reservoirs of creativity within that required me to dig deep within myself.


A profound lesson echoed through the year: the importance of self-care and cherishing those who reciprocate love. It became clear that life's necessitates prioritizing personal happiness over immersion in work. This realization became a guiding principle, reshaping my approach to both academics and life.


Enrolling in the Intro to Programming for Apple Products course proved to be a valuable decision. While drawing didn't resonate as the most enjoyable activity for me, the true highlight emerged in the creation of a podcast with my peers, Kayron and Amber. Click "Podcast" at the top to check it out. This collaborative endeavor allowed me to connect with classmates, share life experiences, offer advice, and break through the walls of reserve that had encased me for too long.


As the semester concludes, so does my journey at FAMU. This profound chapter has been enriched by the unwavering support of my family and the friendships forged along the way. To FAMU, I extend heartfelt gratitude for equipping me with the tools for success.


Onward to the next chapter, embracing the lessons learned and carrying the spirit of creativity and self-discovery into the future.
