Beyond The Set

Welcome to Beyond The Set with Kayron, Amber and Ja'Niyah! In this podcast we take you on a journey through the ups and downs of life outside of the classroom during your college years. Join us as we share personal anecdotes, experiences, and insights on the rollercoaster ride of young adulthood.
